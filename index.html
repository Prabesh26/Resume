Avocado Toast

Creamy avocado slices served on top of toasted bread, drizzled with olive oil and sprinkled with salt and pepper.

A colorful stir-fry dish featuring an assortment of vegetables like bell peppers, broccoli, mushrooms, and snap peas, cooked quickly over high heat with a savory sauce made from soy sauce, garlic, and ginger.
